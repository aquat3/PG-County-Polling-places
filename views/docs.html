Getting Started

Getting started with our polling app. Our polling locator app is integrated with three different end points. The first endpoint is connected to the about page and returns the contents of the about page. The second end point is /form_search, this endpoint takes a geocoded address (geocoded by the Google MAps Platform's geocoding API) and returns a list of nearby polling places that are within a 1-mile radius from the request address. The third endpoint, /doc which returns the contents of the documentation page.

How to use

This is how you use the polling locator website. The first step is to type your address into the search bar that is located on the homepage of the polling site application. The address that you type into the search bar can be your home address or the address of your current location but ensure that the address is a PG county address in order to get the best polling location results.

The second step is to choose one of the search suggested or autocompleted addresses that you are typing into the search bar. Once you on click on your desired address and click the search button the polling site locator will generate about two or three nearest polling locations that are 1 mile from the address you searched for. Each one of the recommended polling locations that is 1 mile from your address has helpul information like the full address of the actual polling location for navigation purposes and a map that pin points the exact location of each polling site. Now all you have to do is choose one of the polling sites the website recommended for you so that you can go there and cast your vote.

Why to use

There are many benefits to using our polling app. The goal is for our polling locator system to provide PG county voters with a reliable way to quickly find their nearest polling sites to help them plan ahead of time and make their voting process a little easier. Our team believes that our system can help increase the voter turnout rate in PG county. We know how important voting is in terms of shaping democracy in the United States that is why residents need a reliable way of presenting that information in this age of unreliable data and fake news.

This polling application is designed to help users quickly find and search up to three open and available polling sites located anywhere in Prince Georges County Maryland that they can immediately go to and cast their votes if they wish. Our team made this application because new and current residents of Prince Georges County residents wanted to have a reliable source in helping them find the right polling places so this app should meet all their needs. We designed this application based on the information that we collected from the conversations we had with new and current PG county residents. So our application was designed based on residents' demands and the opinions they have about the difficulties of finding a reliable service that provides accurate polling sites that also integrates address and map features. We included full addresses and a map that shows the user exactly where their polling place is located by using the prince Georges county map we acquired from Google. When the user types in their address into the search bar they get two or three recommended polling locations that are nearest to them based on their home address or current location address in PG county. Users are given the freedom to choose any of the three recommended polling locations that are generated after the press search and they can also choose another polling location other than the ones our application recommends they choose from.

Elderly voters at PG county that aren't very familiar with online polling site locators will find this application useful and very easy to use. The interface is designed to suit almost all types of users. Individuals that are not used to the internet that want to look up their polling locations will not find it difficult to utilize this polling app since it's free of bloatware like advertisements and it's only designed to handle polling location searches and nothing else. Another reason to use this app is because it was designed based on the feedback we received from PG county college students. Most students are usually busy with work and school life so this app will enable them to quickly lookup the nearest polling sites that are closest to their current location like their college campus for example. overall this polling place locator is supposed to provide you with a fast and a reliable service.
